Salty foods

Pizza dough

Into a bowl 2 teaspoon of yeast and 1 cup and a ⅓ of warm water Add 3 cups of all purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Mix until firm Add onto a floured counter top and need it for 10 mins Put into a lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let it rest for 1 hour and half. Now you can make you pizza To store, put in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap and put in the fridge.

Spicy ground beef

4 tablespoons of ground meat 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper ⅛ tablespoon of salt or aromat 1 tablespoon of chilly oil Break apart meat during cooking Cook until brown and no sign of raw meat.
