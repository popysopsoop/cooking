Pizza dough.

Into a bowl, 2 teaspoons of yeast, 1 cup, and ⅓ of warm water Add 3 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
Mix until firm
Add onto a floured countertop and knead it for 10 mins
Put into a lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rest for 1 and a half hours.
Now you can make your pizza
To store, put in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap, and put in the fridge.
